About the Game

Kardashev is an inventive strategy game where you compete to build the most advanced space civilization by expanding your territory, researching new technology, and conquering your foes. Play as one of six cultures, each with their own unique strengths.

*The Kardashev scale is used to measure the technological advancement of a civilization.
